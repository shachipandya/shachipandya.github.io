So today I turn 25. As every single birthday after the 22nd one is, this one is also special. The number didn't matter when I was in the 'teens' but after grad school, every single one feels important, valuable and memory-bearer. So many things happened in the last year since I wrote [this](http://hardik.org/blog/on-turning-24/) - good, bad and awesome. And I'm thankful for all of those for they helped shape me as a wee bit more experienced and a more mature person. This is my first birthday post-marriage and hence it's a special one.

So for this coming year in life, I do not have outrageous wishes, just the simple usual stuff. And I would be very satisfied if I managed to fulfill all of it.

* Work even more.
* Take even more photographs.
* Travel even more.
* Love even more.
* Ditch anger.
